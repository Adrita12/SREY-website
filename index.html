ABOUT US

St. Thomas College proudly presents SREY 2K24, Kolkata's premier on-campus tech festival.

This immersive experience offers a dynamic platform for aspiring innovators and tech enthusiasts. Engage in thought-provoking events, insightful interactions, and electrifying competitions designed to challenge and inspire. Network with knowledgable minds, connect with fellow tech aficionados, and gain firsthand exposure to cutting-edge advancements.

SREY 2K24 ignites your passion for technology, sparks innovation, and empowers you to shape the future alongside the brightest minds. Join us and be wired, inspired, and empowered!
